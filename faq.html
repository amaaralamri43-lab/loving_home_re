Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our services, facilities, and booking process.

What should I bring when boarding my dog?

We recommend bringing your dog's favourite toy, their regular food, and any required medication. Bedding is optional as we provide soft, hygienic bedding in every suite.

Are your staff trained in animal care?

Yes. All staff members are trained in dog behaviour, grooming safety, and emergency care, ensuring a safe and professional experience for every pet.

Can I visit the facilities before booking?

Absolutely. We encourage all pet owners to book a guided visit so you can explore our suites, play areas, and wellness services.

How often will my dog be walked?

This depends on the package you choose. All dogs receive regular exercise, enrichment, and supervised outdoor time throughout the day.

Do you offer grooming during the stay?

Yes. Our professional grooming spa is available for all dogs staying with us. Some packages include a free grooming session.
